why bips?

Tools for everyone

We wanted to make brain imaging easier. Run workflows on the server or download and install bips locally on your machines.

Live results

When integrated with databases, you will be able to compare across sites and studies and provide dynamic views of results.

Tagged and searchable

How do you find the workflow you need? Every workflow is tagged with keywords. This makes them easily searchable.

Integrated provenance

Tools accepted into the framework will provide standardized provenance using the W3C PROV model.

Expanding services

BIPS will provide an increasing collection of services ranging from annotation, to metadata query to actually running workflows.

Standardization

The goal of BIPS is to conform to evolving standards (e.g., XCEDE-DM, XCEDE-O) and support integration with many brain imaging databases and other resources
